# Requirements Document: Sikshya-Sathi System

## Introduction

Sikshya-Sathi (SS) is an offline-first agentic tutor system designed for rural Nepali K-12 students. The system employs a two-brain architecture: a Cloud Brain powered by Amazon Bedrock Agent for personalization and content generation, and a Local Brain that runs offline on student devices for content delivery and performance tracking. The system addresses the unique challenges of rural Nepal's infrastructure through periodic synchronization and compressed learning bundles.

## Glossary

- **Sikshya_Sathi_System**: The complete educational platform including Cloud Brain and Local Brain
- **Cloud_Brain**: Amazon Bedrock Agent-powered component responsible for personalization, content generation, and curriculum integration
- **Local_Brain**: Offline-capable component running on student devices for content delivery and tracking
- **Learning_Bundle**: Compressed package containing lessons, quizzes, hints, and study materials synchronized from Cloud to Local Brain
- **MCP_Server**: Model Context Protocol server providing Nepal K-12 curriculum integration
- **Sync_Session**: Periodic data exchange between Local Brain and Cloud Brain
- **Study_Track**: Personalized learning path generated by Cloud Brain based on student performance
- **Performance_Log**: Local Brain record of student interactions, quiz results, and learning progress
- **Adaptive_Rule**: Local Brain logic for adjusting content difficulty without generating new content
- **Curriculum_Validator**: Component ensuring content alignment with Nepal K-12 curriculum standards

## Requirements

### Requirement 1: Problem Definition and Target Users

**User Story:** As a rural Nepali student, I want access to personalized AI tutoring that works offline, so that I can learn effectively despite unreliable internet connectivity.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Sikshya_Sathi_System SHALL support students in grades K-12
2. THE Sikshya_Sathi_System SHALL cover core subjects including Mathematics, Science, Nepali, English, and Social Studies
3. THE Sikshya_Sathi_System SHALL operate on low-cost Android devices with minimum 2GB RAM
4. WHEN internet connectivity is unavailable, THE Local_Brain SHALL continue delivering educational content
5. THE Sikshya_Sathi_System SHALL accommodate rural Nepal infrastructure constraints including intermittent power and limited bandwidth

### Requirement 2: Cloud Brain Capabilities

**User Story:** As the Cloud Brain, I want to generate personalized learning content and study plans, so that each student receives instruction tailored to their needs and curriculum requirements.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Cloud_Brain SHALL generate personalized lessons based on student performance data
2. THE Cloud_Brain SHALL create quizzes aligned with Nepal K-12 curriculum standards
3. THE Cloud_Brain SHALL generate contextual hints for quiz questions and problem-solving
4. THE Cloud_Brain SHALL create revision plans based on student knowledge gaps
5. THE Cloud_Brain SHALL generate study tracks that adapt to individual learning pace
6. WHEN generating content, THE Cloud_Brain SHALL integrate with the MCP_Server for curriculum alignment
7. THE Cloud_Brain SHALL use Amazon Bedrock Agent as the personalization engine
8. THE Cloud_Brain SHALL package generated content into compressed Learning_Bundles
9. WHEN student performance data is received, THE Cloud_Brain SHALL analyze patterns and adjust personalization strategies
10. THE Cloud_Brain SHALL validate all generated content against Nepal K-12 curriculum standards before packaging

### Requirement 3: Local Brain Capabilities

**User Story:** As the Local Brain, I want to deliver educational content offline and track student progress, so that learning continues uninterrupted regardless of connectivity.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Local_Brain SHALL deliver lessons from synchronized Learning_Bundles without internet connectivity
2. THE Local_Brain SHALL administer quizzes and record student responses locally
3. THE Local_Brain SHALL track student performance including completion rates, accuracy, and time spent
4. THE Local_Brain SHALL store Performance_Logs locally until next Sync_Session
5. THE Local_Brain SHALL apply Adaptive_Rules to adjust content difficulty based on performance
6. THE Local_Brain SHALL NOT generate new educational content
7. WHEN a quiz is completed, THE Local_Brain SHALL provide immediate feedback using pre-synchronized hints
8. WHEN Learning_Bundles are depleted, THE Local_Brain SHALL notify the student to sync when connectivity is available
9. THE Local_Brain SHALL maintain a local cache of at least 2 weeks of learning content
10. THE Local_Brain SHALL operate with maximum 100MB memory footprint during active use

### Requirement 4: Synchronization Model

**User Story:** As a system administrator, I want efficient periodic synchronization between Local and Cloud Brains, so that personalization improves while minimizing bandwidth usage.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN internet connectivity is available, THE Sikshya_Sathi_System SHALL initiate a Sync_Session
2. WHEN a Sync_Session occurs, THE Local_Brain SHALL upload Performance_Logs to the Cloud_Brain
3. WHEN a Sync_Session occurs, THE Cloud_Brain SHALL download new Learning_Bundles to the Local_Brain
4. THE Sikshya_Sathi_System SHALL compress Learning_Bundles to maximum 5MB per week of content
5. THE Sikshya_Sathi_System SHALL complete a full Sync_Session within 2 minutes on 2G connectivity
6. WHEN a Sync_Session is interrupted, THE Sikshya_Sathi_System SHALL resume from the last successful checkpoint
7. THE Sikshya_Sathi_System SHALL prioritize critical content updates during partial syncs
8. THE Sikshya_Sathi_System SHALL validate data integrity after each Sync_Session
9. WHEN sync fails after 3 attempts, THE Local_Brain SHALL continue operating with existing content

### Requirement 5: Personalization Model

**User Story:** As an educator, I want the system to personalize learning at topic, skill, and cognitive levels, so that each student receives appropriately challenging content.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Cloud_Brain SHALL personalize content at the topic level based on curriculum units
2. THE Cloud_Brain SHALL personalize content at the skill level including comprehension, application, and analysis
3. THE Cloud_Brain SHALL personalize content at the cognitive level using Bloom's taxonomy
4. WHEN a student demonstrates mastery, THE Cloud_Brain SHALL advance to more challenging topics
5. WHEN a student struggles, THE Cloud_Brain SHALL provide additional practice and scaffolding
6. THE Cloud_Brain SHALL maintain a student knowledge model tracking proficiency across topics
7. THE Cloud_Brain SHALL generate study tracks that balance review and new content
8. THE Cloud_Brain SHALL adapt pacing based on student learning velocity
9. THE Local_Brain SHALL apply Adaptive_Rules to select appropriate content from Learning_Bundles based on recent performance

### Requirement 6: Content Quality and Curriculum Alignment

**User Story:** As a curriculum coordinator, I want all content validated against Nepal K-12 standards, so that students receive accurate and appropriate instruction.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Cloud_Brain SHALL validate all generated content using the Curriculum_Validator
2. THE Curriculum_Validator SHALL verify alignment with Nepal K-12 curriculum standards
3. THE Curriculum_Validator SHALL check content for age-appropriateness
4. THE Curriculum_Validator SHALL verify language appropriateness for Nepali students
5. WHEN content fails validation, THE Cloud_Brain SHALL regenerate until standards are met
6. THE MCP_Server SHALL provide authoritative curriculum reference data
7. THE Cloud_Brain SHALL tag all content with curriculum standard identifiers
8. THE Sikshya_Sathi_System SHALL maintain an audit log of content validation results
9. WHEN generating quizzes, THE Cloud_Brain SHALL ensure questions map to specific learning objectives

### Requirement 7: Safety and Content Filtering

**User Story:** As a parent, I want the system to provide safe, age-appropriate content, so that my child learns in a protected environment.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Cloud_Brain SHALL filter all generated content for inappropriate material
2. THE Cloud_Brain SHALL prevent generation of harmful, violent, or adult content
3. THE Cloud_Brain SHALL validate cultural appropriateness for Nepali context
4. WHEN unsafe content is detected, THE Cloud_Brain SHALL reject and regenerate
5. THE Sikshya_Sathi_System SHALL log all content filtering events for audit
6. THE Cloud_Brain SHALL implement guardrails preventing prompt injection attacks
7. THE Local_Brain SHALL not accept content without valid Cloud_Brain signatures
8. THE Sikshya_Sathi_System SHALL provide parental controls for content access

### Requirement 8: Performance and Reliability

**User Story:** As a student with limited device resources, I want the system to respond quickly and reliably, so that my learning experience is smooth and frustration-free.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Local_Brain SHALL respond to user interactions within 200ms
2. THE Local_Brain SHALL load lessons within 1 second
3. THE Local_Brain SHALL render quizzes within 500ms
4. THE Cloud_Brain SHALL generate a Learning_Bundle within 30 seconds
5. THE Sikshya_Sathi_System SHALL maintain 99% uptime for Local_Brain operations
6. WHEN device battery is below 15%, THE Local_Brain SHALL enter power-saving mode
7. THE Local_Brain SHALL gracefully handle device interruptions including calls and notifications
8. THE Local_Brain SHALL persist student progress every 30 seconds to prevent data loss
9. WHEN the Local_Brain crashes, THE Sikshya_Sathi_System SHALL recover to the last saved state
10. THE Cloud_Brain SHALL handle concurrent requests from up to 10,000 students

### Requirement 9: Data Privacy and Security

**User Story:** As a student, I want my learning data protected, so that my privacy is maintained and my information is secure.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Sikshya_Sathi_System SHALL encrypt all Performance_Logs during storage and transmission
2. THE Sikshya_Sathi_System SHALL anonymize student data before analytics processing
3. THE Cloud_Brain SHALL store student data in compliance with Nepal data protection regulations
4. THE Sikshya_Sathi_System SHALL require authentication before accessing student profiles
5. WHEN a Sync_Session occurs, THE Sikshya_Sathi_System SHALL use TLS 1.3 for data transmission
6. THE Local_Brain SHALL not share student data with third parties
7. THE Sikshya_Sathi_System SHALL provide data export functionality for students and parents
8. THE Sikshya_Sathi_System SHALL allow students to delete their learning history
9. THE Cloud_Brain SHALL implement role-based access control for educators and administrators

### Requirement 10: High-Level Architecture

**User Story:** As a system architect, I want clear separation between Cloud and Local Brain responsibilities, so that the system is maintainable and scalable.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Sikshya_Sathi_System SHALL implement a two-brain architecture with Cloud_Brain and Local_Brain
2. THE Cloud_Brain SHALL be responsible for content generation, personalization, and analytics
3. THE Local_Brain SHALL be responsible for content delivery, tracking, and offline operation
4. THE Cloud_Brain SHALL use Amazon Bedrock Agent for AI capabilities
5. THE Cloud_Brain SHALL integrate with MCP_Server for curriculum data
6. THE Local_Brain SHALL implement a local database for content and performance storage
7. THE Sikshya_Sathi_System SHALL use a RESTful API for Cloud-Local communication
8. WHEN components are updated, THE Sikshya_Sathi_System SHALL maintain backward compatibility for at least 2 versions
9. THE Sikshya_Sathi_System SHALL support horizontal scaling of Cloud_Brain components

### Requirement 11: Risk Mitigation

**User Story:** As a project manager, I want identified risks mitigated through technical solutions, so that deployment succeeds in rural Nepal conditions.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN internet connectivity is unreliable, THE Local_Brain SHALL operate independently for at least 2 weeks
2. WHEN device storage is limited, THE Sikshya_Sathi_System SHALL compress content to minimum viable size
3. WHEN Cloud_Brain generates incorrect content, THE Curriculum_Validator SHALL reject before distribution
4. WHEN sync bandwidth is constrained, THE Sikshya_Sathi_System SHALL use delta synchronization
5. WHEN device performance is poor, THE Local_Brain SHALL degrade gracefully with simplified UI
6. WHEN power is intermittent, THE Local_Brain SHALL implement aggressive state persistence
7. THE Sikshya_Sathi_System SHALL provide offline fallback content for critical learning paths
8. WHEN Cloud_Brain is unavailable, THE Local_Brain SHALL continue with last synchronized content
9. THE Sikshya_Sathi_System SHALL implement circuit breakers for external service dependencies

### Requirement 12: Success Metrics and Monitoring

**User Story:** As an education program director, I want measurable success metrics, so that I can evaluate system effectiveness and learning outcomes.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Sikshya_Sathi_System SHALL track student learning improvement through pre/post assessments
2. THE Sikshya_Sathi_System SHALL measure daily active usage and engagement time
3. THE Sikshya_Sathi_System SHALL track quiz completion rates and accuracy trends
4. THE Sikshya_Sathi_System SHALL measure content coverage across curriculum standards
5. THE Sikshya_Sathi_System SHALL track sync success rates and bandwidth usage
6. THE Cloud_Brain SHALL generate weekly progress reports for students and educators
7. THE Sikshya_Sathi_System SHALL measure time-to-mastery for curriculum topics
8. THE Sikshya_Sathi_System SHALL track student retention and dropout rates
9. THE Sikshya_Sathi_System SHALL provide dashboards for educators showing class-level analytics
10. THE Cloud_Brain SHALL identify struggling students for intervention

### Requirement 13: Phased Deployment Roadmap

**User Story:** As a deployment lead, I want a phased rollout strategy, so that we can validate the system before full-scale deployment.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Sikshya_Sathi_System SHALL implement an MVP phase with core offline delivery and basic personalization
2. THE MVP SHALL support one subject (Mathematics) for grades 6-8
3. THE MVP SHALL validate offline-first architecture and sync mechanisms
4. THE Sikshya_Sathi_System SHALL implement a Pilot phase with 3 schools and 500 students
5. THE Pilot SHALL expand to all core subjects for grades 6-8
6. THE Pilot SHALL validate curriculum alignment and learning outcomes
7. THE Sikshya_Sathi_System SHALL implement a Scale phase supporting all K-12 grades
8. THE Scale phase SHALL support 50+ schools and 10,000+ students
9. WHEN each phase completes, THE Sikshya_Sathi_System SHALL undergo evaluation before proceeding
10. THE Sikshya_Sathi_System SHALL maintain backward compatibility during phase transitions

### Requirement 14: Educator and Administrator Tools

**User Story:** As a teacher, I want tools to monitor student progress and customize learning paths, so that I can provide effective guidance and support.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Cloud_Brain SHALL provide a web dashboard for educators to view student progress
2. THE Cloud_Brain SHALL allow educators to assign specific topics or study tracks
3. THE Cloud_Brain SHALL generate class-level performance reports
4. THE Cloud_Brain SHALL identify students needing additional support
5. THE Cloud_Brain SHALL allow educators to review and approve generated content
6. THE Cloud_Brain SHALL provide curriculum coverage reports by class and student
7. WHEN an educator modifies a study track, THE Cloud_Brain SHALL update the next Learning_Bundle
8. THE Cloud_Brain SHALL allow administrators to configure system parameters
9. THE Cloud_Brain SHALL provide usage analytics for program evaluation

### Requirement 15: Localization and Accessibility

**User Story:** As a Nepali student, I want content in my language with appropriate cultural context, so that I can learn effectively in my native environment.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Sikshya_Sathi_System SHALL support Nepali and English language interfaces
2. THE Cloud_Brain SHALL generate content appropriate for Nepali cultural context
3. THE Local_Brain SHALL support Unicode Devanagari script rendering
4. THE Sikshya_Sathi_System SHALL provide audio support for students with reading difficulties
5. THE Local_Brain SHALL support text size adjustment for visual accessibility
6. THE Sikshya_Sathi_System SHALL use examples and contexts relevant to rural Nepal
7. WHEN generating content, THE Cloud_Brain SHALL use Nepali curriculum terminology
8. THE Local_Brain SHALL support offline text-to-speech for accessibility
9. THE Sikshya_Sathi_System SHALL provide simplified UI mode for younger students
